# SESHSURE — PRIVACY POLICY (DRAFT — ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED BEFORE PUBLICATION)

**Vido Manufacturing and Distribution Corp d/b/a SeshSure** · Effective date: [DATE]

## What we collect
- **Business contact data**: names, work emails, phone numbers, titles of company representatives.
- **Account & transaction data**: orders, invoices, payment records, bank account details for ACH (account numbers stored encrypted; card data is handled by our payment processor and never stored on our systems).
- **Signature evidence**: typed signatures, timestamps, IP addresses, and device information when agreements are signed.
- **Usage data**: log-ins, pages viewed (including invoice-view records), and system logs, collected for security, audit, and evidence purposes.
- **Prospect data**: information you submit through our contact forms, including how you heard about us.

## How we use it
To operate the Hub: process orders and payments, send invoices and operational notices, track shipments, maintain legally required records, enforce agreements (including collections), prevent fraud, and improve the service. We send operational email (invoices, reminders, receipts) as part of the service; marketing email includes an unsubscribe option and honors CAN-SPAM.

## What we share
Service providers only, and only as needed to operate: banking partners (ACH origination), payment processor (cards), email delivery, shipment tracking, and cloud hosting. We do not sell personal information. We may disclose records when required by law, or in legal proceedings to enforce our agreements (e.g., statements of account and signature evidence filed with a court).

## Retention
Transaction and signature records are retained as long as needed for legal, tax, and audit purposes (generally at least seven years). You may request deletion of prospect data that is not tied to a transaction at any time.

## Security
Role-based access controls, row-level database security, encrypted transport, encrypted storage of bank account numbers, and audit logging of administrative actions.

## Your choices & rights
Colorado residents may have rights under the Colorado Privacy Act; note that the CPA generally exempts B2B and commercial-context data, but we honor reasonable access and correction requests regardless. Contact rob@seshsure.com.
