# MANUFACTURING & SERVICES AGREEMENT (DRAFT — ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED BEFORE USE)

Between **Vido Manufacturing and Distribution Corp d/b/a SeshSure** ("SeshSure"), Parker, Colorado, USA, and **[FACTORY LEGAL NAME]** ("Manufacturer"), [COUNTRY].

## 1. Scope
Manufacturer will produce pre-roll cones and related goods to SeshSure's written specifications ("Specs"), and may provide separately identified services (tooling, artwork setup, quality inspection, packing) as ordered. Each production run is initiated through the SeshSure Hub and confirmed by Manufacturer with a promised ship date.

## 2. Specifications, QC & Acceptance
Goods must conform to the acknowledged Spec version for the run. Manufacturer will apply lot numbers to all cartons and retain QC records per lot for 3 years, producing them on request. Non-conforming goods identified through SeshSure's dispute process, where the root cause is ruled Manufacturer fault, will be remedied at Manufacturer's cost by replacement run (materials and freight at Manufacturer's expense) or credit, at SeshSure's election.

## 3. Pricing, Invoicing & Payment
Prices are per-unit in USD as recorded in the Hub (rate card or awarded bid). Manufacturer submits statements through the Hub; lines that deviate from agreed rates are flagged for resolution before settlement. Payment terms: [NET 15 / NET 30 / NET 45 / ON SHIPMENT] with [X] bps early-payment discount if paid within [Y] days. Settled statement lines are final.

## 4. Customs Valuation — COMPLIANCE CLAUSE (IMPORTANT)
Commercial invoices for goods shipped to the United States must state the **full transaction value actually paid or payable for the goods**, consistent with 19 U.S.C. § 1401a. Charges for bona fide services that are properly excludable or separately dutiable may be separately invoiced **only where they genuinely reflect services rendered and are documented as such**. The parties expressly agree that **no invoice will understate the value of goods** (for example, nominal "goods" pricing with value shifted to "service" fees). Both parties acknowledge that undervaluation exposes the importer to penalties under 19 U.S.C. § 1592 and is prohibited under this Agreement. If the parties wish to reduce dutiable value lawfully, they will evaluate a documented **first-sale-for-export structure** with customs counsel before implementation. Manufacturer will cooperate with reasonable documentation requests supporting declared values, country-of-origin marking, and HTS classification (currently 4813.10.0000 for blank cone paper products).

## 5. Delivery, Risk & Freight

Manufacturer provides complete, accurate shipping documents (commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin where applicable) before vessel departure. ## 6. Intellectual Property
All Specs, artwork, tooling designs, and SeshSure trademarks remain SeshSure's exclusive property. Manufacturer receives a limited license solely to produce for SeshSure. **Manufacturer will not produce SeshSure-designed or patented products for any third party**, nor use SeshSure tooling or artwork for any other customer, during the term and for [3] years after. Patented flagship products may be produced only upon separate written flagship approval.

## 7. Confidentiality & Non-Circumvention
Each party keeps the other's non-public information confidential. Manufacturer will not directly or indirectly solicit or transact with SeshSure's customers introduced through this relationship, during the term and for [3] years after.

## 8. Compliance
Manufacturer represents compliance with applicable labor, safety, and environmental laws, and that goods contain no prohibited substances. Manufacturer will provide lot-level Certificates of Analysis where the Spec requires.

## 9. Term & Termination
