# SOP 05 — DEMAND LETTER
**Trigger:** ⚖️ drafted task (30d+ overdue, no plan, no open dispute).
**Steps:** 1) Open client page → letter panel. 2) Verify the invoice list and total against the statement. 3) Edit tone/facts if needed — the machine drafts, you sign. 4) Channel: BOTH for anything you may litigate — email for speed, certified for the green card. 5) Approve & send; print + mail same day if certified. 6) Staple the green card to the file when it returns.
**Done when:** status APPROVED_SENT; deadline task appears at +10 days.
**Next step if unpaid at deadline:** payment plan offer or court packet → Douglas County filing.
